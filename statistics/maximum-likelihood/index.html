Maximum likelihood estimator (MLE) is method of finding the estimators of unknow parameters that maximized the probability of the know outcome samples.

That is, what value of θ\thetaθ that gave us the maximum probability (If it's continuous, then think of it dxdxdx as very small, that is the instantanuous probability of getting an exact outcome of x1,x2,...x_1, x_2,...x1​,x2​,...)
